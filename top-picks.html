TOP PICKS

This page is a quick overview of my top five favorite meals I've had! Hover over an image to get the address of the location!

Joy Yee Noodle

Joy Yee offers a wide range of choices on their menu. While this can be intimidating for a newcomer, you can never go wrong with the Spicy Shrimp XO Noodles. They're the perfect amount of spicy with a good amount of peppers and onions to add a good change in texture. If you're ever at Joy Yee, you have to give it a try!

Soban Korea

The beef bibimbap bowl at Soban Korea is incredible! Unexpectedly, it came in with a wide choice of mix-ins so you could customize it to your liking. I've ordered beef bibimbap many places and have not had this option before, so it was a really nice change. Soban Korea's prices are very reasonable as well. Not pictured, they also had a "Samanko" ice cream sandwich with a red bean flavoring which was the perfect amount of sweet after a great meal. Couldn't recommend it enough!

Yolk Test Kitchen

Yolk Test Kitchen is known for their unconventional flavor combinations. I must admit, when seeing a "buffalo chicken and mac-n-cheese waffle" on their menu, I was hesitant. However upon trying it, it was my new favorite breakfast place! It's the perfect mix of flavors-- you get a bit of everything. Sweet, spicy, and salty, this order can satisfy any craving!

Yatai Street Food

If you should know anything about me-- I'm a huge poke bowl fan. When I ordered this from Yatai, I was a little surprised to see that the bowl looked a bit plain at first glance. I'm used to sauces and toppings overflowing, so I wasn't sure what to expect when I saw this in front of me. However, I was surprised with how amazing the quality of the fish was, and the flavor really shone when there wasn't much covering it up. It's definitely a different poke experience for me, and it opened my eyes to how little really goes a long way!

Alexander's Breakfast & Lunch

A sweet brunch is always the best start to your day, and this churro french toast was heavenly. A soft middle, crunchy edge coated with cinnamon sugar was perfectly balanced with the fresh fruit and mascarpone on top. The presentation was also great, I couldn't wait to dive in right when it arrived to the table!
